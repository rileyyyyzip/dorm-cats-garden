Artist Statement

Dorm Cats Garden is a cozy interactive tribute to the resilient stray cats that inhabit university dormitories. In real-life student dorms, stray cats occupy a unique emotional space—patrolling the lawns, sunbathing under streetlamps, and accepting snacks from stressed students during final exams. They are the unofficial therapists and campus mascots, providing a touch of nature and comfort to those living far from home.

This game captures that warm connection. The tiny green sprout (reminiscent of Pikmin) resting on each cat's head represents the seeds of comfort, healing, and green life they plant in our hearts. By caring for the campus lawn, leaving fish cookies, and watching these chubby cartoon blobs waddle around, we relive the quiet daily rituals of student caretakers who find joy in these small, whiskered encounters.
